Tory Jordan.

Tory J Harder is a professional Colorist at Vanishing Angle, with 8 years of experience in the film and television industry. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Digital Film & Video Production from The Art Institute of California - San Francisco, which has provided him with a strong foundation in the industry.

Tory has a proven track record of delivering high-quality results in color grading, with a deep understanding of techniques and an expert proficiency in DaVinci Resolve. He is a multi-talented individual with a range of skills and a passion for creativity, and he also brings over a decade of experience in video editing.

At Vanishing Angle, Tory has made significant contributions to the company as a key member of the in-house editorial team, playing a critical role in various projects and initiatives. His focus on collaboration, innovation, and client satisfaction has helped to ensure the successful delivery of high-quality work.

Outside of work, Tory is a passionate photographer, web developer, and the occasional video gamer. He is dedicated to personal growth and community involvement, always striving to make a positive impact in both his personal and professional life.
