Tory Jordan.

Tory Jordan is a professional Colorist at Vanishing Angle, with 9 years of experience in the film and television industry. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Digital Film & Video Production from The Art Institute of California - San Francisco, which has provided him with a strong foundation in the industry.

Tory has collaborated with renowned clients such as Grey Goose Vodka, VTech Toys, DirecTV, Hulu, Lowe's Home improvement, and Red Bull. As a key member of the in-house editorial team at Vanishing Angle, he has made valuable contributions to various projects and initiatives.

When he's not working, Tory indulges his passion for photography, web development, riding motorcycles, and the occasional video game. Additionally, he takes pride in being an uncle to 4 nephews, valuing his role in their lives.
