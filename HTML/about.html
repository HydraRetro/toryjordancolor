TORY JORDAN

Tory J. Harder is a Colorist for Digital Film & Television. His work has been featured on Netflix, Amazon Prime, YouTube Red, Lowe's Home Improvement, FedEx, AT&T, DirecTV, Delta Airlines, M&M's, Trojan Brand, and VTech Toys. In 2016, He was Lead Editor for the YouTube Red series, MatPats Game Lab. He is currently employed at the Los Angeles-based production company, Vanishing Angle as their in-house editorial.

Tory received his B.S. in Digital Filmmaking & Video Production from The Art Institute of California - San Francisco.
